Welcome to Reynolds House Inn

The Reynolds House, built 1892 by G.J. Reynolds, is a recently restored and beautiful historic home located in the heart of Barre City, Vermont. We are currently taking reservations for Cleora's Carriage House which has spacious room for meetings, parties, and other events. It has a full service bar and internet available.
